BIPCLUSION

13.02.2024. - 13.05.2024.

About

The Blended Intensive Program (BIP) offers participants a unique opportunity to collaborate internationally on various topics. Spanning from February 13th to May 13th, 2024, participants engage in virtual and mobile teamwork targeting skill improvement in software development.


Participants are devided in 3 teams:

  • 3D Printing
  • App Design
  • Entrepreneurship


In virtual mobility each team is provided with a task whose outcome will then be presented in the mobile part of project.
The particpants will be joint in the main team of interculturality which is important as it fosters mutual understanding, respect, and cooperation among people from diverse cultural backgrounds, ultimately promoting social cohesion while working in a team.
